Navient Solutions Complaints: A Breakdown

As Navient Solutions, LLC came under fire for taking advantage of student loan borrowers, there was a renewed focus on the history of complaints against the organization. According to data from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, there have been more than five thousand complaints lodged against the company over the course of the last three years. Upon further examination, the vast majority of these complaints fell under the umbrella of student loan-based complaints and credit reporting. This did not come as a surprise considering Navient’s status as one of the nation’s largest student loan servicers.

When breaking down the complaints by state, it was clear that users of the service across the country were taken advantage of. By and large, the number of complaints in a state were correlated with the state’s population. Some of the nation’s most populous states in California (1st in population), Florida (3rd in population), Texas (2nd in population) and New York (4th in population) lodged the most complaints. While it wasn’t a one-to-one correlation, the strong positive relationship shows that the nation as a whole was taken advantage of as opposed to specific states or populations.
